Reaction images

This page is not meant to be frequently visited, but rather serves as a tutorial to use this feature. The frontend above to get random images is purely for asthetics and experimenting.
The idea is to have a keyboard shortcut that, when pressed, automatically loads a random image of a particular theme into your clipboard, to be pasted anywhere.
The images are retrieved from www.lekkerveld.be/get-random-image and placed in your clipboard. To implement this on your own device, make a shell script with the following content, and link a keyboard shortcut to it:

#!/bin/bash
curl https://www.lekkerveld.be/get-random-image?\&imgtype=ANGERY | xclip -selection clipboard -target image/png -i

imgtype=ANGERY means the image will be from the ANGERY theme. If you want anything else, replace ANGERY with one of the other options.
